Tips for renting apartments

Try to avoid intermediaries - agencies and private realtors. Agents in Italy are a whole sect and those who have already rented apartments in this way will tell you more than one example of how they were eventually deceived. I have dozens of stories like this.

To protect yourself from trouble, we recommend renting apartments only through the official services of Booking.com and Airbnb.com.

If you need an apartment for a long time, then a great idea would be to rent it first for a month on Airbnb, take a closer look, and then negotiate with the owner directly - this way you will save several thousand euros on the agency commission. I personally used this method many times and recommend it to everyone.

With the help of special filter links in the articles, you can choose the best accommodation option for you with the highest ratings based on real traveler reviews.

In the comments to the relevant articles in this section, you can ask questions about rental housing. I try to respond to all comments at least once a day.
